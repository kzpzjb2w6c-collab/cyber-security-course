# x) Summary of Traficom's Cybermeter: National framework for the assessment of cybersecurity capabilities
## Assesment process (Traficom, 10)
1. The process starts when a management team decides to starts and sets goals for the assessment. A sponsor and facilitator is appointed for the process.
2. The sponsor and facilitator decide together on which operating areas should be assessed and critical depedencies like systems, processes, and data assets. Based on those decisions, they identify key stakeholders from the organisation and agree on the methodology and schedule.
